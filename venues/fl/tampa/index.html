Golf Simulators in Tampa, FL

12 venues

Tampa's growing sports and entertainment scene includes a solid selection of indoor golf venues, concentrated in downtown and the Channel District.

Whether you’re a seasoned pro or simply looking for a fun activity, Tampa’s simulator venues offer a perfect year-round golfing escape. Expect realistic virtual courses and the chance to practice your swing in comfort, regardless of the weather. These facilities are incredibly versatile, making them ideal for casual date nights, fun outings with friends, or dedicated practice for serious golfers looking to improve their game.

Pricing is generally accessible, with most simulator sessions ranging from $30 to $60 per hour. You will find options to suit every budget and skill level. 🎯 Simulator Brand Guide — What's the Difference?

Trackman
Radar-based. Most accurate. Tour-level data. Used by Five Iron Golf.
Full Swing
Camera + radar hybrid. Used by Tiger Woods. Huge course library.
X-Golf
Photo-electric sensors. Very realistic ball flight. Popular franchise model.
TruGolf E6
Best graphics. Budget-friendly. Great for casual players and entertainment venues.
SkyTrak
High accuracy at mid-price. Common in private setups and smaller venues.
Foresight GC3
Camera-based. High accuracy. Popular in dedicated golf facilities.
