Golf Simulator Brand Guide

Not all golf simulators are equal. Here's what you need to know about the major simulator brands — from tour-level accuracy to budget-friendly entertainment setups.

TRACK MAN
Trackman
Most AccurateTour-LevelPremium Price

Radar-based dual-radar technology. Used by PGA Tour pros for practice and fitting. Tracks 26+ data points on every shot. The gold standard for serious golfers. Used by Five Iron Golf across all locations.

FULL SWING
Full Swing
Camera + RadarTiger's Choice

Camera and radar hybrid system. Used by Tiger Woods at his home. Massive course library with 100+ licensed courses. Great visuals and accuracy. Popular in upscale bar and lounge settings.

X-GOLF
X-Golf
FranchiseRealistic Feel

Photo-electric sensor system with very realistic ball flight. Consistent experience across 200+ franchise locations in 30+ states. Great for social golf and competitive leagues alike.

TRUGOLF E6
TruGolf E6
Best GraphicsBudget-Friendly

Best-in-class graphics and course visuals. Great for casual players, entertainment venues, and bars. Lower price point makes it common in neighborhood spots and smaller venues.

SKY TRAK
SkyTrak
High AccuracyMid-Price

Photometric camera system with high accuracy at a mid-tier price point. Common in private home setups and smaller commercial venues. Good for practice and casual play.

FORESIGHT GC3
Foresight GC3
Camera-BasedHigh Accuracy

Three high-speed cameras track club and ball data with excellent accuracy. Popular in dedicated golf performance centers and fitting studios. Trusted by club fitters and instructors.
