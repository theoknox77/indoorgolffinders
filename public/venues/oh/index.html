🎯 Simulator Brand Guide — What's the Difference?

Trackman
Radar-based. Most accurate. Tour-level data. Used by Five Iron Golf.
Full Swing
Camera + radar hybrid. Used by Tiger Woods. Huge course library.
X-Golf
Photo-electric sensors. Very realistic ball flight. Popular franchise model.
TruGolf E6
Best graphics. Budget-friendly. Great for casual players and entertainment venues.
SkyTrak
High accuracy at mid-price. Common in private setups and smaller venues.
Foresight GC3
Camera-based. High accuracy. Popular in dedicated golf facilities.
