Frequently Asked Questions

How many indoor golf simulator venues are in Oklahoma?

IndoorGolfFinders lists 36 indoor golf simulator venues across Oklahoma. These span 12 cities and include dedicated golf bays, simulator bars, golf lounges, and training facilities.

What are the most popular golf simulator brands in Oklahoma?

The most common commercial golf simulator brands found in Oklahoma venues include TrackMan, Full Swing, Foresight GCQuad, SkyTrak, and X-Golf. Availability varies by venue — check individual listings on IndoorGolfFinders for confirmed simulator brand information.

How much does it cost to use a golf simulator in Oklahoma?

Golf simulator rentals in Oklahoma typically range from $25 to $60 per hour per bay. Many venues offer membership plans for frequent players. Prices vary by simulator brand, time of day, and specific location.
