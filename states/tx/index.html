Frequently Asked Questions

How many indoor golf simulator venues are in Texas?

SimFind lists 222 indoor golf simulator venues across Texas. These span 86 cities and include dedicated golf bays, simulator bars, golf lounges, and training facilities.

What are the most popular golf simulator brands in Texas?

The most common commercial golf simulator brands found in Texas venues include TrackMan, Full Swing, Foresight GCQuad, SkyTrak, and X-Golf. Availability varies by venue — check individual listings on SimFind for confirmed simulator brand information.

How much does it cost to use a golf simulator in Texas?

Golf simulator rentals in Texas typically range from $25 to $60 per hour per bay. Many venues offer membership plans for frequent players. Prices vary by simulator brand, time of day, and specific location.
