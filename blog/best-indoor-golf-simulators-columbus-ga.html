Understanding Today's Simulator Technology

Modern golf simulators use advanced launch monitor technology to capture every detail of your swing. The best systems in Columbus use industry-leading brands that provide accurate ball flight data, spin rates, and distance measurements. When you step up to hit a ball on one of these simulators, sophisticated cameras and sensors track your club head speed, launch angle, and ball trajectory in real-time.

The leading simulator brands you'll find at Columbus venues include:

  • TrackMan: Professional-grade technology used on tour, offering precise data on every shot
  • Full Swing: Known for realistic graphics and accurate physics simulation
  • Foresight GCQuad: Ultra-precise tracking system that captures detailed ball and club data
  • SkyTrak: Popular for home and commercial use, providing excellent accuracy at a reasonable price point
  • Bushnell Launch Pro: Compact but powerful, delivering reliable swing analysis
  • Toptracer: Accessible technology that's becoming increasingly common in venues

Each system has its strengths, but all provide the data you need to understand your game better and track improvement over time.
