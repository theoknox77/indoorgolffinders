Why Indoor Golf Simulators Are Game-Changers

Modern golf simulators have transformed how players improve. Unlike traditional driving ranges, simulators give you immediate, detailed feedback on every shot. You'll see metrics like ball speed, launch angle, spin rate, and carry distance. This data is invaluable for identifying swing issues and tracking progress over time.

Norfolk's indoor facilities feature top-tier simulator technology from brands like TrackMan, Full Swing, Foresight GCQuad, SkyTrak, Bushnell Launch Pro, and Toptracer. Each system has its strengths, but all provide accurate shot tracking and access to hundreds of golf courses worldwide.

Beyond practice, simulators let you play full 18-hole rounds on famous courses. Want to test yourself on Pebble Beach or Augusta National? You can do it indoors in Norfolk, regardless of season or time of day. This makes golf accessible when outdoor play isn't practical.
