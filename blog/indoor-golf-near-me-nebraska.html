What to Expect from Modern Golf Simulators

Today's indoor golf simulators are nothing like the outdated systems from years past. Modern venues across Nebraska feature professional-grade technology that serious golfers trust.

You'll find several leading brands at Nebraska facilities:

  • TrackMan — The gold standard used by PGA Tour professionals, TrackMan captures every aspect of your swing with unmatched accuracy
  • Full Swing — Known for realistic graphics and a vast library of playable courses worldwide
  • Foresight GCQuad — Delivers precise ball data and swing analytics for serious improvement

Finding the Right Venue for Your Game

Nebraska's indoor golf venues vary in size, technology, and atmosphere. Some facilities focus primarily on serious golfers and swing analysis, while others emphasize fun and entertainment for casual players and groups.
