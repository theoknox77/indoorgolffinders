Understanding Indoor Golf Simulator Technology

Before you head out to find indoor golf near you, it helps to understand what you're getting into. Today's top-tier simulators use sophisticated technology to track your ball flight, club data, and swing mechanics with incredible precision.

The most common brands you'll encounter in Modesto venues include:
