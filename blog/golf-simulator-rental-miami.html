Foresight's GCQuad simulator uses a combination of radar and camera technology to track the ball's flight, providing an incredibly accurate reading. SkyTrak simulators, meanwhile, use a Doppler radar system to track the ball's speed and distance, making it a popular choice among golf enthusiasts.

In addition to the brand of the simulator, you'll also want to consider the accuracy of the system.
