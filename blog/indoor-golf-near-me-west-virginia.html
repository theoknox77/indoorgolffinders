What to Expect from Modern Golf Simulators

Today's indoor golf simulators are far more advanced than the golf games you might remember from entertainment centers. Real golf simulators use sophisticated launch monitor technology to track every detail of your swing—ball speed, launch angle, spin rate, and more—then display accurate shot results on a screen.

The most popular simulator brands you'll find in West Virginia facilities include:

  • TrackMan – The gold standard in professional golf, used by PGA Tour players and top instructors for its exceptional accuracy and detailed analytics
  • Full Swing – Known for immersive graphics and a massive course library that lets you play famous courses worldwide
  • Foresight GCQuad – Offers excellent ball-tracking technology and works well for both casual players and serious golfers
  • SkyTrak – A more affordable option that still delivers reliable data for practice and entertainment
  • Bushnell Launch Pro – Provides solid performance metrics at a mid-range price point
  • Toptracer – Great for range practice and tracking improvement over time

Each platform has its strengths, so trying different simulators can help you find your preferred experience.
