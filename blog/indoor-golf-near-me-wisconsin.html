Top Simulator Technology Available in Wisconsin

When you're searching for indoor golf near you, it helps to understand the technology behind the simulators. Wisconsin venues feature several industry-leading systems that provide accurate ball tracking and realistic course conditions.

  • TrackMan - Known for its precision launch monitors, TrackMan systems provide detailed swing metrics and are used by professional golfers worldwide. You'll find TrackMan technology at premium facilities across Wisconsin.
  • Full Swing - Full Swing simulators offer immersive visual experiences with high-quality graphics and accurate ball physics. Many Wisconsin venues feature Full Swing setups for both casual play and competitive leagues.
  • Foresight GCQuad - This advanced launch monitor captures essential swing data and delivers realistic gameplay. GCQuad systems are increasingly popular at Wisconsin's top-tier indoor golf facilities.
  • SkyTrak - A more accessible option that doesn't compromise on accuracy, SkyTrak systems are common at mid-range facilities throughout the state.
  • Bushnell Launch Pro - Offering excellent value and reliable performance, Bushnell Launch Pro systems are found at facilities across Wisconsin.
  • Toptracer - Originally developed by golf broadcast technology experts, Toptracer brings range-quality ball tracking to indoor venues.

Each system has its strengths, and many Wisconsin facilities operate multiple brands to give you options based on your preferences and goals.
