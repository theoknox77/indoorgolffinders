Understanding Indoor Golf Simulator Technology

Modern indoor golf simulators use sophisticated sensor systems to capture your swing and deliver immediate, actionable feedback. The leading brands you'll find in Newark facilities include:

  • TrackMan: Industry gold standard offering precise ball flight tracking and detailed swing metrics
  • Full Swing: Delivers immersive 4K graphics with extensive course library and entertainment features
  • Foresight GCQuad: Advanced launch monitor providing club and ball data for serious swing analysis
  • SkyTrak: Affordable option perfect for casual players and home enthusiasts seeking quality performance
  • Bushnell Launch Pro: Compact yet powerful system offering excellent accuracy for swing coaching
  • Toptracer: Popular choice combining entertainment value with legitimate practice capabilities

Each system captures different metrics including ball speed, launch angle, spin rate, and club face data. This information helps you identify swing flaws, track progress over time, and make data-driven improvements to your game.
