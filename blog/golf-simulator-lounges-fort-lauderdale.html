Top Simulator Technology You'll Find Locally

Fort Lauderdale's premier golf lounges invest in the best launch monitors available. Here's what you should know about the technology you'll encounter:

  • TrackMan - Trusted by PGA Tour professionals, TrackMan provides incredibly accurate ball flight data and swing analysis. You'll get precise measurements of carry distance, spin rate, and launch angle.
  • Full Swing - Known for immersive graphics and authentic course experiences, Full Swing simulators make you feel like you're actually playing Pebble Beach or St. Andrews.
  • Foresight GCQuad - Offers superior accuracy for swing coaching and serious improvement, with detailed metrics that help golfers understand their technique.
  • SkyTrak - A popular choice for upscale lounges, SkyTrak combines affordability with excellent accuracy and a massive library of playable courses.
  • Bushnell Launch Pro - Great for casual players and practice, this launch monitor delivers solid data at venues focused on fun and entertainment.
  • Toptracer - Perfect for range practice and skill games, Toptracer lets you compete in virtual challenges and improve specific aspects of your game.

When you visit a Fort Lauderdale simulator lounge, ask which launch monitors they use. Different technologies suit different goals, whether you're working with an instructor, playing social rounds, or practicing your short game.
